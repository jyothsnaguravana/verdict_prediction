About

This Project deals with predicting the verdict of the given case filed in the court.The Supreme court of India has launched "SUPACE" (Supreme Court Portal for Assistance in Courts Efficiency) to collect and analyse the data ,"SUVAS" (Supreme Court Vidhik Anuvaad Software) to translate legal papers from English into vernacular languages and vice-versa and many other to automate the work in judicial system.There are around 41861760 pending cases in supreme court ,in which one-third are civil cases which can be dealt within less court sessions. Hence this project dealts with predicting the verdict by infering the previous cases which are similar to the case filed ,evidences provided and also displays the amendaments by which the punishment is given to the culprit. Using natural language processing ,the documents filed are parsed and given to the model previously built by feeding past hearings and the output is generated. The client under the consultation of lawyer can fill all the details to the court website and from there it can be given to the model when he/she is scheduled. Due to this automation there would be transparency in judiciary, thereby making the court system timely and optimal.
